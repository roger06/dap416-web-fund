Bonjour Bootstrap

Resize the browser window to see what happens.

col-lg-3 col-md-4 col-sm-6 col-xs-12
col-lg-6 col-md-4 col-sm-6 col-xs-12
col-lg-3 col-md-4 d-md-block d-sm-none col-xs-12
The above gives us:
  • 3 / 6 / 3 at large width
  • 4 / 4 / 4 at medium width
  • 6 / 6 at small width (third col disappears)
  • 12 / 12 / 12 at extra small width (stacked vertically)

You do NOT need to use @media queries with Bootstrap!
